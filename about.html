Biography

I am an Ukrainian electric and double bass player, based in Kyiv, who was fortunate to play as a sideman with many others jazz artists. I was born in Rogatyn in Western Ukraine. My family moved to Ivano-Frankivsk at first, and then to Kyiv, therefore I grew up in those two cities. I am mostly self-taught musician, although I had been taking private lessons and have been attending workshops for jazz musicians as well. Here are some of them:

  • "Cho-jazz" (Chodzie≈º, Poland);
  • "Lviv Summer Jazz School" (Lviv, Ukraine);
  • "New York Jazz Masters"(Trzebnica, Poland).

I graduated from'Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv' in 2006, where I studied history, but shortly after that, music became my only activity.

Performance experience

I mostly perform with Dennis Adu's Qiuntet and Big band and Dmitriy Aleksandrov's Quartet. Also have been collaborated with: Alexander Maksymiw; Alexander Pavlov; A Banda Brasil; Bogdan Kravchuk Quartet; Nelson Carrasco and Ajiaco Son; Danil Zverkhanovsky; Stanislav Chumakov; Bogdan Gumenyuk; Ruslan Yegorov.
